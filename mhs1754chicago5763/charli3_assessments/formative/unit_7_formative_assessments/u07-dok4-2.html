Unit 7 DOK 4.2 Design a Safe Household Circuit — V1
Name:
Version 1
Show work and mark answers clearly.
Context: Bedroom circuit.

Students build or critique a complete model for the bedroom circuit context. They must use diagrams/data, relevant formulas, circuit principles, and evidence to defend a corrected design or explanation.

  1. Define the system or circuit being analyzed.
  2. Use the provided evidence to build or revise the model.
  3. Apply formulas or circuit principles where useful.
  4. Defend the final design/explanation and address one likely misconception.
  5. Compare series and parallel behavior using current paths and equivalent resistance.
