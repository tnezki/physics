Unit 7 DOK 4.4 Critique a Circuit Claim — V1
Name:
Version 1
Show work and mark answers clearly.
Context: Current gets used up.

Students build or critique a complete model for the current gets used up context. They must use diagrams/data, relevant formulas, circuit principles, and evidence to defend a corrected design or explanation.

  1. Define the system or circuit being analyzed.
  2. Use the provided evidence to build or revise the model.
  3. Apply formulas or circuit principles where useful.
  4. Defend the final design/explanation and address one likely misconception.
